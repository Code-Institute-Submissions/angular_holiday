Accommodation Options

Emerald Travel Montserrat offers two accommodation options for vacationers traveling with us. A more affordable but still luxurious shared option and a slightly more expensive private option. The stunning shared four and five bedroom villas enjoy ocean and mountain views, communal living spaces and swimming pools. Each bedroom is accompanied with a private ensuite bathroom. If you prefer something a little more private Emerald Travel works closely with some of the hotels in the area which we will be happy to book for you at no extra cost.

Mango Falls

  • Located right above the waters of the Caribbean Sea! The place where Princess Anne stayed when she visited Montserrat in 2005
  • The veranda runs the entire length of the house, and the bedrooms are separated for maximum privacy.
  • The pool is surrounded by rain forest, banana trees and lush foliage on one side and the ocean on the other.
  • Shy prehistoric-looking iguanas sun themselves in the yard providing great photo opportunities.

Wing-Song

  • A really spectacular and very private home with amazing sea and mountain views.
  • Open plan living space and kitchen diner.
  • Wrap around veranda, with 6 steps to pool.
  • Three bedrooms and baths (2 on main level, one lower level) have AC, gorgeous bathrooms.
  • Beautifully decorated, BBQ, WIFI, many extras
